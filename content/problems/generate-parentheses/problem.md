Given `n` pairs of parentheses, return **all combinations of well-formed
parentheses** of length `2n`. The combinations may be returned in any order.

## Constraints
- `0 <= n <= 8`.

## Examples
Input: `n = 3`
Output: `["((()))","(()())","(())()","()(())","()()()"]`

Input: `n = 1`
Output: `["()"]`

Input: `n = 0`
Output: `[""]`
