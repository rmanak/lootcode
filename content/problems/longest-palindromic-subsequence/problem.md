Return the length of the **longest palindromic subsequence** of `s`.

## Constraints
- `1 <= len(s) <= 1000`
- `s` is lowercase letters

## Examples
Input: `s = "bbbab"`
Output: `4`
Explanation: `bbbb` is a palindromic subsequence.

Input: `s = "cbbd"`
Output: `2`
Explanation: `bb` is the longest palindromic subsequence.
