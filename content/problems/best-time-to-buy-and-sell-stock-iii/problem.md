`prices[i]` is the stock price on day `i`. **Return the maximum profit** with at
most **two** transactions (you must sell before buying again).

**Examples**
```
prices = [3,3,5,0,0,3,1,4]  ->  6
prices = [1,2,3,4,5]        ->  4
prices = [7,6,4,3,1]        ->  0
```

**Constraints:** `0 <= len(prices) <= 10^5`, `0 <= prices[i] <= 10^5`.
