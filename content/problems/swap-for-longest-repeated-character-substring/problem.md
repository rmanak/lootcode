You may swap two characters of `text` at most once (or not at all). **Return the
length of the longest substring of a single repeated character** achievable.

**Examples**
```
text = "ababa"    ->  3
text = "aaabaaa"  ->  6
text = "aaabbaaa" ->  4
text = "aaaaa"    ->  5
```

**Constraints:** `1 <= len(text) <= 2*10^4`, lowercase letters.
